Easy Peasy 5 Minuites Korean Kimchi by Sol Moon

Description

  • A traditional Kimchi in the easiest way that you'd ever find on the internet!

  • A korean delicacy that can be made with ingredients that you can easily buy at a supermarket.

Ingredients

  • 1 Korean bechu

  • 6 galick cloves

  • 3 medium size carrots

  • 1 big spoon of gochujang

  • 1 ginger

  • 2L salty water

Steps

  1. Put the bechu in salty water for at least one hour.

  2. Blend the rest of ingredients. Add a bit of salty water to make it easier for blender to work.

  3. mix them all!
