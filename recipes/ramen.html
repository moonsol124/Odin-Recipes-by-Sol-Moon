Spicy Ramen Recipe by Sol Moon

Description

  • Spicy buldak ramen with vegies

  • An easy meal that you can prepare within few minuites

Ingredients

  • 1 buldak ramen

  • half onion

  • 50g of spinach

  • 1 egg

  • 1 cheese string

  • 50g broccoli

Steps

  1. Cut the vegies in easily eatable size pieces and throw them in a boiling water with ramen noodles

  2. When fully cooked, incorporate the buldak sauce into the ramen and mix it.

  3. put a string of cheese, a runny egg or whatever that you want for the topping!
